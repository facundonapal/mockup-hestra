Sport Classic

Our biggest source of inspiration in the Sport Classic line is nature — the wild and unrestrained characterizes both the design and the selection of materials. Typical choices are elk leather from the Nordic and North American deerskin, lined with warm wool or lambskin.

glove making

Our gloves in the Table Cut line are handmade in a small workshop in Hungary in accordance with the French tradition of glove making, a method that has remained unsurpassed since THE 17th century.

about hestra

Hestra gloves is a family company in the third and fourth generation. We have been making gloves of the highest quality since our establishment in 1936. Within the family we have the only two active glove cutters in Scandinavia.
